Services We Provided

We are providing following services

  • Custom Wix Website Development

    We design and build stunning, responsive websites using Wix and Editor X. Every site is tailored to your brand and optimized for performance across all devices.

  • Advanced Velo Development

    Unlock the full potential of Wix with advanced Velo scripting. From dynamic forms to interactive features, we bring complex ideas to life using JavaScript and Wix’s backend.

  • Third-Party API Integration

    We connect your site to external platforms like Stripe, Google Sheets, CRMs, or messaging services. Automate workflows and extend functionality seamlessly.

  • E-Commerce & Booking Solutions

    Enhance your Wix Stores, Bookings, Events, or Hotels with custom features. We add smart filters, flexible checkout flows, automated confirmations, and tailored booking experiences.

  • CMS & Member Area Customization

    We build scalable content systems using Wix CMS and secure member areas with custom user portals. Easily manage blogs, directories, dashboards, or gated content without code.

  • SEO, Performance & Analytics Setup

    Your site will be optimized for search engines, speed, and tracking. We handle meta tags, schema, SEO setup, and integrate tools like Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel
